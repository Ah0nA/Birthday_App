A BRIEF HISTORY ON AKAN NAMING

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born. These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person. Middle names have considerably more variety and can refer to their birth order, twin status, or an ancestor's middle name.But we will focus on the first name for now.
The table below simplifies the Akan naming system based on day of birth and gender.

DAY BORN MALE NAMES FEMALE NAMES
Sunday Kwasi Akosua
Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
Tuesday Kwabena Abena
Wednesday Kwaku Akua
Thursday Yaw Yaa
Friday Kofi Afia
Saturday Kwame Ama
